Our Purpose

Why PERMA exists.

PERMA was founded on a quiet but powerful conviction: that no one should be left behind. For 50 years we have served Malaysians of every background — with urgency, dignity, and long-term commitment.

Our work is guided by the belief that dignity is not a privilege. It is a right.

Our History

Five decades of care.

From a small founding team to a national network — the milestones that shaped PERMA.

1976

PERMA is Founded

A small team with a clear conviction — serve all Malaysians facing hardship, without condition.

1980s

Welfare Programmes Expand

Structured food aid, financial support, and crisis response formalised as core offerings.

1990s

Health Initiatives Launched

Screening, prevention, and mental wellbeing support introduced — caring for the whole person.

2000s

Capacity Building Takes Root

Education, skills training, sports, and the arts — building opportunity, not just relief.

Today

50 Years Strong

A nationwide network — still showing up, still committed, still growing.

What We Do

Three pillars, one mission.

PERMA's work is structured around three core areas — each essential to building communities where no one is left behind.

Donate Now → Volunteer
01
Core Pillar One

Welfare

When hardship strikes, PERMA responds quickly and without condition. Our welfare programmes deliver immediate, practical support to those who need it most.

🍱

Food Aid

Regular meal distribution and food basket programmes for families facing food insecurity.

💰

Financial Assistance

Emergency support for essential needs — rent, utilities, medical bills, and more.

🏠

Crisis Response

Rapid deployment during acute crises, including floods, fires, and sudden family emergencies.

👴

Elderly & Differently-Abled Care

Targeted welfare for elderly individuals and those with disabilities who face additional barriers.

12K+
Families supported through welfare programmes annually
Early detection
saves lives — our screenings reach thousands who would otherwise go undiagnosed
02
Core Pillar Two

Health

PERMA believes prevention is as important as cure. Our health programmes focus on early detection, community education, and mental wellbeing.

🩺

Health Screenings

Free community screenings for chronic conditions including diabetes, hypertension, and cancer.

🧠

Mental Wellbeing

Counselling, support groups, and awareness programmes addressing mental health needs.

📚

Health Education

Community workshops on nutrition, hygiene, reproductive health, and disease prevention.

👶

Child & Maternal Health

Support programmes focused on healthy development from early childhood through adolescence.

03
Core Pillar Three

Capacity Building

Lasting change requires more than immediate relief. PERMA invests in education, skills, sports, and the arts — creating pathways beyond hardship.

🎓

Education Support

Scholarships, tutoring, and school supply programmes that keep children in education.

🛠️

Vocational Skills Training

Practical skills in trades, technology, and entrepreneurship that open doors to sustainable livelihoods.

⚽

Sports Programmes

Community sports initiatives that build discipline, teamwork, and confidence in young people.

🎨

Arts & Culture

Creative programmes that foster expression, identity, and community pride — especially for youth.

Long-term
Our capacity building programmes create change that outlasts any single act of assistance
