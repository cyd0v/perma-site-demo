About PERMA

The Heart of PERMA

We believe no one should be left behind. Every programme we run is built on compassion, dignity, and practical support that helps people move forward.

Our History

How the journey grew.

From a small founding effort in 1976 to a trusted organisation serving communities across Malaysia.

1976

PERMA was founded with a mission to support vulnerable communities.

1980s

Welfare aid programmes expanded to support more families in need.

1990s

Health screenings and community wellbeing initiatives were introduced.

2000s

Capacity building through education and skills programmes took root.

Today

A nationwide network still committed to dignity, care, and inclusion.

Purpose · Vision · Mission

Purpose

To ensure individuals and families facing hardship are not overlooked, through timely and compassionate support.

Vision

Nurturing minds through skills development, education and sports.

Mission

To empower young minds through education and capacity-building programmes.

Community Welfare

Reaching Those In Need

At PERMA, we believe that no one should be left behind. Through our community welfare initiatives, we provide timely assistance to individuals and families facing hardship, offering support where it is needed most. Whether through food aid, essential supplies, financial assistance, or community outreach programmes, our goal is to bring hope, restore dignity, and improve the well-being of vulnerable communities.

Every act of kindness, no matter how small, helps build a stronger, more compassionate society.

PERMA Welfare Programme

❤️ Health & Well-being

Promoting healthier lives through medical assistance, screenings and mental wellness initiatives.

  • Medical Assistance
  • Health Screening Programmes
  • Mental Wellness Support
  • Community Health Awareness

✦ Impact

Transforming lives through early intervention and access to care, enabling individuals to lead healthier lives.

Meet Prasana

A Second Chance In Life

Thanks to a mammogram screening arranged by PERMA, my breast cancer was detected early. Prompt treatment saved my life and today I am living a healthy and normal life. I am grateful for this lifesaving programme and the compassion PERMA showed me during a difficult time.

Prasana a/p Sivaprasad

📚 Education

Providing educational opportunities and mentorship to children and youth from underserved communities.

  • Future Leaders Nurtured
  • Providing education resources and support
  • Mentorship Programmes
  • Sports & Leadership Development

✦ Impact

Empowering young people to learn, grow and achieve their full potential. Creating brighter futures.

Meet Karthik

Stepping Towards A Brighter Future

When I was nine years old, I underwent surgery to correct a deformity in my legs. During my recovery I had to stay home for several months and was worried that I would fall behind in school. PERMA supported me with a tablet which allowed me to continue attending online classes from home.

Today, I have made a full recovery and am now thriving in Form 1. I am grateful to PERMA and everyone who believed in me. Their kindness, encouragement and timely support gave me the opportunity to continue learning and build a brighter future.

Karthik a/l Sivakumar

  • Families Supported

  • Life Skills Programmes

✦ Impact

Building resilient families, creating pathways to self-reliance and independence.

VTOC Graduate

Journey to Independence

Through VTOC's Culinary Programme, Norfaizah transformed her passion for cooking into a career. After graduating, she was immediately employed by Genting Malaysia Berhad as a Service Crew in the F&B sector. Her success is a testament to how vocational education empowers individuals to achieve financial independence and a brighter future.

Norfaizah a/p Singli
